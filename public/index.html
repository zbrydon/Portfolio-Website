About Me

I am a 21-year-old third year Deakin University Software Engineering student. Throughout my degree, I have undertaken numerous projects developing software, IoT, mobile, web and robotics applications. While completing these projects I have learned to work independently, lead teams, solve problems, de-bug and meet strict project deadlines with specific technical requirements. The Software Engineering faculty has fostered an encouraging yet independent working environment, stimulating me to become an efficient and self-motivated student, which I hope to translate to my professional work.

My part time work experience as team supervisor and member in multiple departments at Woolworths has helped me to develop a customer-oriented mindset, working effectively to achieve and surpass my duties whilst prioritising customer satisfaction. I feel a sense of loyalty and pride in my workplace, having been at Woolworths for 4 years, and will apply this same attitude towards my career.

My hobbies include basketball, surfing, and fitness as I enjoy maintaining a healthy, balanced, and regimented lifestyle. However, I am most passionate about architecting multifaceted IoT systems. I have an analytical brain and enjoy dissecting the intricacies of designing systems to be holistic, user centric and fault tolerant, while ensuring the solution is efficient. I am truly zealous when it comes to both university and personal projects and always give them my all. I hope to apply this enthusiasm to part-time employment or a summer internship to allow me to begin a career as a Software Engineer that I am truly passionate about.
